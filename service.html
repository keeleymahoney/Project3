BEDS

BEDS is a community organization that provides services to local homeless individuals. They provide food, housing, connect them to jobs, and create a community. The 12th Can

The 12th Can is an on campus food pantry that works to eliminate hunger in the Texas A&M Community. Each month, the 12th Can hosts four openings for students, staff and faculty to pick out groceries. Volunteers work to check in clients, stock the pantry, and ensure that everything is running smoothly. I really enjoyed seeing the impact that this work was making on the lives of the local community.
